Game rules
Initial position
  • Starting amount
    Each player is dealt 10 marbles at the start
  • Objective
    Objective of the game is to collect all of your opponentâ€™s marbles
  • Roles
    The are only 2 players (i.e./ 'Hider' and 'Guesser')
Flow of the game
  • Turns
    Each round Hider has to pick an odd or even number from his stack => and Guesser has to do the same. Guesser's objective is to correctly predict if Hider's number is odd or even. He can wager any number he wants
  • Outcome
    The prize is determined by the amount of marbles Guesser wagered
  • Marbles left
    If one of the Players is left with only 1 marble he can only assume the Guesser role. When a player has no marbles left, he loses
Example

If the Guesser is correct, he collects the amount of marbles he wagered (e.g. Hider chose 3 marbles, Guesser picked 2 => Guesser collects 2 marbles)

If the Guesser is incorrect => Hider collects the amount of marbles wagered by the Guesser (e.g. Hider pick odd, Guesser wagers 8 and guesses incorrectly => loses 8 marbles)
